About

During my career in the medical field I saw the massive potential (and need) for workforce multiplication through digital tools (like EMRs and Patient interface portals). I saw these tools as a way to not only streamline the interaction between patients and their care teams but in doing so also improve the quality of care which patients were receiving in general. Communication is key to maintaining an up to date, accurate medical history for a patient, which directly translates in to better overall care. Having a patient portal with an intuitive, accessible interface is critical in recruiting patient participation in their own care! I saw many of the same issues once I begin work as a district manager for a medical device manufacturer. The apps and digital tools available for internal communication and customer relations management were excellent. However the ability for customers to reach out and recieve the details for our education events, product specifications and even pricing information was all but non-existent and relied basically on old-school mass email blasts, which of course are less than ideal. I always wanted an outward facing solution for customers to easily access intructional videos and product information when they needed a quick refresher. So I went and took a course on Full-Stack development and now I want to create sleek, responsive business solutions for you!
